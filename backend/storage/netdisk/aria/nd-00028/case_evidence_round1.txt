- Description: Three (3) pages of dot-matrix printer output, continuous feed paper with perforated edges removed. Content appears to be fragmented financial figures and project codenames.
- Chain of Custody: Found interleaved within unrelated technical manuals in a common-area recycling bin, Building C, first floor. Retrieved by custodial staff on 13 November 2003, approx. 07:00 EST and forwarded to security.
- Initial Observation: Paper shows minimal handling wear. Text is fragmented but includes repeated alphanumeric strings (e.g., "Zephyr-7", "Tango Actual"). Recommended for latent fingerprint analysis and content cataloging.

--- PRELIMINARY ASSESSMENT ---

This first-round collection establishes a baseline of disparate but potentially correlative data points. The floppy disk (CE-044-01) and the hard-copy documents (CE-044-03) suggest an attempt to utilize outdated or low-visibility mediums, possibly for off-record communication or data transfer. The network traffic anomalies (CE-044-02) provide a digital thread that may link specific user terminals to the activity implied by the physical evidence. Immediate next steps should focus on forensic recovery of the floppy disk contents and correlating the IP addresses from the packet capture with employee access schedules and workstation assignments for the period in question. All items are securely stored in the main evidence locker (Locker 12) pending further analysis.
