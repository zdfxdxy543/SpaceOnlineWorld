--- PRELIMINARY ASSESSMENT ---
Evidence suggests a coordinated incident with both digital and physical vectors. The floppy disk (E-01) is anachronistic and requires priority analysis. The failed logins from a decommissioned IP (E-02) indicate possible credential spoofing or internal network compromise. The environmental anomaly (E-03) may correlate with unauthorized hardware activity or a deliberate attempt to trigger a system fault. The witness statement (E-04) loosely corroborates the timeline. Immediate next steps: Forensic image of E-01, deep audit of network traffic for IP 192.168.6.12, and full review of all server room access logs for the past 48 hours.
